My name is Daniela Archibong. I have always had an interest in design, internet and programming. My first computer was the PREcomputer1000 that came with a manual on learning BASIC programming.

In junior high, I owned my own domain that allowed me to design 'web cliques' and host other people interested in creating their own website. I joined the afterschool club, 'TechnoHawks', just so I had faster internet I could use to upload files through my FTP server. Throughout high school, I took digital imaging classes and learned how to manipulate photos through photoshop, illustrator, etc.

After years of technical support roles, I realized my passion is in knowing how things work and building projects. I am actively working on re-learning what I taught myself in the past as well as expanding my knowledge in various programming languages. Also, I am working on learning the various tools that are used in group projects in a professional environment.

Most recently, I am teaching myself how to use the terminal in Linux to work on as many projects as I can. I am working on a Ubuntu 18.04LTS setup on my Lenovo IdeaPad. I am also attending classes at Austin Coding Academy.
